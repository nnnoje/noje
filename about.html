KARTELL' EM IS A LIFESTYLE BRAND THAT CELEBRATES THE VARIETY OF OUR SOCIETY'S SUBCULTURES. WITH OUR CLOTHING, WE HOPE TO INSPIRE EVERYONE BY DELIVERING IMPORTANT MESSAGES AND SHOWCASING OUR BRAND.

WE SEE OURSELVES AS BEING MORE THAN JUST A CLOTHING LINE. OUR GOAL IS TO BECOME A CULTURAL MOVEMENT THAT HONORS UNIQUENESS, PERSONAL STYLE, AND THE POTENTIAL OF STREET FASHION AS AN ARTISTIC MEDIUM.

KARTELL' EM IS A FILIPINO HIP-HOP COLLECTIVE CONSISTING OF MEMBERS WAIIAN, NE7IN, YORKO, SICA, WAYVIER, RUIIJIKUN, NICKNAME, ONEEN, AFTERMATT, JWUDZ, AND IDSG. MOST OF THE MEMBERS RESIDE IN MAKATI, ALTHOUGH SOME OF THE OTHERS ARE SCATTERED AROUND THE REST OF METRO MANILA.
